Black Swan / Cygnet

Jae Hyun Lee | Summer 2026

Abstract

Black Swan DAO is a Berlin based decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) founded by Penny Rafferty, Calum Bowden, and Laura Lotti. The founding members of the DAO are researchers, visual theorists, and developers, exploring the relationship between technological, economic, and cultural systems. The digital initiative was birthed out in democratize institutional assets, such as equipment, expertise and guidance, funding, space, audiences, and publicity. By coordinating voting system through Web3.0 software, Black Swan DAO utilizes the network of its community, comprised of artists, designers, technologists, and ecologists.
