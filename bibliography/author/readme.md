//Black Swan DAO is a Berlin based decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) founded by Penny Rafferty, Calum Bowden, and Laura Lotti. The founding members of the DAO are researchers, visual theorists, and developers, exploring the relationship between technological, economic, and cultural systems. The digital initiative was birthed out in attempt to democratize institutional assets, such as equipment, expertise and guidance, funding, space, audiences, and publicity. Black Swan DAO, as a platform, embodies the principles of transparency not only in capital transaction but also in governance through blockchain technology. By coordinating voting system through their Web3.0 software (Cygnet app), Black Swan DAO utilizes the network of its community, comprised of artists, designers, technologists, and ecologists.
